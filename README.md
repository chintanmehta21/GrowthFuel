## âœ¨ Features

- **Personalized Dashboard:**
  - See your workout and habit scores at a glance
  - Weekly workout stats and calories burned chart
  - Friendly greeting and quick navigation

- **Workout Logging:**
  - Log exercises by category (Push, Pull, Legs, Upper, Lower)
  - Add sets, weights, and reps with ease
  - Track progress over time

- **Habit Tracking:**
  - Create custom habits (Durational, Read, Task, Count)
  - Daily reset and completion tracking
  - Visualize your streaks and progress

- **History & Analytics:**
  - Daily, weekly, and monthly views
  - Calendar with workout/habit indicators
  - Detailed stats and trends

- **Profile & Settings:**
  - Manage your profile, preferences, and dark mode
  - Secure authentication (email/password, Google)

- **Real-time Sync:**
  - All data is securely stored and synced with Supabase
